Here our are top 5 2 hour knitting kits! These are perfect for a lazy sunday evening or keep your hands busy whilst watching your favorite movie. Our 2 hour knit projects are also great to take on holiday or even just to keep handy for a moment of selfish, guilt free me-time.  
At WATG we believe strongly in Me time, which in 'modern' times may be hard to find. Between work, family and general life stealing yourself 2 hours a week might become a bit of challenge. To make the most of your selfish hours why not knit up something wooly as an extra treat. Knitting is proven to be relaxing and meditative...so put your feet up, grab a cuppa and start one of our top 5 quick-knit projects.
Yummy Scarf -  Incase you're new to knitting the yummy scarf is super easy, you just use 2 balls our Crazy Sexy Wool and the stocking stitch. This over sized scarf is a cool way to update any winter coat and if you're feeling generous it makes a great present for any loved one. The kit includes everything you need, including the pattern and optional super large 25mm needles.
Rockwell Mittens - These are a treat for cold hands and take around 2 hours from cast on to cast off. We recommend knitting them up using our Wooly Bully Alpaca, a super soft blend of 20% merino and 80% baby alpaca. These are easy to knit up using the simple garter stitch and the twisted rib stitch. The kit includes 2 x balls of Wooly Bully Alpaca, the pattern and the optional 5mm needles.
Lil Foxy Roxy - The Lil' version of our giant foxy roxy scarf. This scarf is really simple to knit up and is a winter classic. Use just one ball of Crazy Sexy Wool and the garter stitch, you can have this great winter warmer knitted up in no time. We also recommend this kit for any beginner knitters. Our Lil' Foxy Roxy knit kit includes everything you will need.
Bag It Up Clutch - Using our Jersey Be Good you can knit yourself up a statement accessory. We love to take our out to dinner or for a saturday stroll at the market. We love our recycled jersey be good yarn, which is made from factory off cuts. These offcuts are made into new yarns buy Peruvian locals! This simple but stylish bag is easy to make and easy to wear. The knit kit includes all of your yarn, the pattern and the option of 10mm needles.
Heart Beanie - Using our Zion Lion as a base the Heart Beanie is sweet take on a WATG classic. After knitting your beanie base you then embroider a heart and add a pom pom! If you're unsure about how to do this you can learn in our Wool School. We use Crazy Sexy Wool for this beanie, but if you wanted you could embroider in Jersey Be Good for extra texture. For this beanie you will need 3 balls of Crazy Sexy Wool which come in the knit kit along with the pattern, sewing needle and optional needles.

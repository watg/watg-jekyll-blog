A History of Houndstooth Why not show off your knowledge as well as your knitting skills. The houndstooth print originates from Scotland, as many classic prints do. It's also known as houndstooth check and dogstooth. First worn by Scottish shepherds the Houndstooth was made popular by upperclass men and women in New York, and became popular again throughout the 60s. Many fashion and knitwear designers use this statement print in their collections, from Chanel to Stella McCartney and...Wool and the Gang.

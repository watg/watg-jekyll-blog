[stag_intro]We're joining forces with West Elm to run parties across the US - join us and get your make on![/stag_intro]

We've got two great Party designs for you to wrap your mitts around.

For those of you who'd like to try a spot of macramė, there's our Top of the Pots plant hanger. It's made from our awesome upcycled Jersey Be Good yarn - learn more about its magical properties here.

If you want to learn to arm knit - it's knitting, but using your arms as needles - we have our Jazz Hands Snood, made from our super soft and chunky Crazy Sexy Wool.

No experience is needed as we'll have an expert Gang Maker on hand at each event to help you get to the finish line. The ticket price of $30 includes all of the materials you'll need to get your make on - and they're yours to take home at the end of the Party.

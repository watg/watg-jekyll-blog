Here at Wool and The Gang we have been busy creating our new woolen baby collection, just in time for your baby’s first winter adventure!

[/stag_intro]

Our new Baby Gang collection comes in sizes 0-6 and 6-12 months, these new pieces make adorable and unique presents for your baby, a friend's baby or even cool knits for your grandchildren.

Our new cozy knits are made using super soft 100% Peruvian Sheepaca and our favorite Sugar Baby Alpaca yarn, which is hypo-allergic and also has thermal properties...perfect for your little one! For newbies our Knit Kit’s come with easy to follow instructions and our online tutorials will teach you how to knit, stitch by stitch. All of our new Baby Gang pieces are also available as ready-made, having been lovingly and sustainably hand knitted by a team of our Gangstas in Peru.

Due to popular demand we have shrunk our beloved Stevie Sweater. Made from Sheepaca, our blend of merino and alpaca wool, The Stevie Sweater Jr will keep even the smallest member of your family snug this winter. The Lazy Day Leggings are another Wool and The Gang favorite, cute and practical our knitted leggings feature a drawstring and tiny turn-ups making them perfect to pair with the new Merci Cardigan, which is stylish and comfy for both rest or play time.

The new baby gang collection also includes adorable easy to knit hats and blankets for beginner knitters plus our Pretty Gal Dress which is a must in any Baby Gang member’s winter wardrobe! Watch out other new Baby Gang woollen goodies on the way.

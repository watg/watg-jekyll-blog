Raise your flippers for a high five - the Gang are just back from London Fashion Week and our fin-tastic collaboration with iconic British designer, Christopher Ræburn. In the ultimate sustainable fashion mash-up, models walked down the runway wearing textured beanies, oversized snoods,  chunky mitts and structured belts - all uniquely designed by WATG x Christopher Ræburn and hand-made by our Gang of knitters.
[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2015/02/Quote.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Our Gang of local makers have been busy knitting up fifty pairs of Bruce KnitMitts for lucky FROW fashionistas, while another fifty lucky show-goers took away Knit Kits to make their own jawesome creations. Back-stage, the air reverberated with the click of knit-stix as we tempted the models and the Christopher Ræburn team to take a time out and get their knit on....
[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2015/02/Bigger_Ball.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

You can swallow up the full collaboration later this year - however if you’re hungry for a lil’ something right now, why not snap up your very own pair of Bruce KnitMitts? Buy ready-made by our UK Gang or knit up a pair with your own fair hands. Our AW15 prediction: you're gonna go wild for this collection - but a word of caution: use your new set of jaws with care!

Have you ever needed to knit a scarf in a flash? Arm knitting is the fastest way to knit, you can make a scarf in less than half an hour! It's fast and easy, no prior knitting experience needed.

In this tutorial we show you how to knit a big chunky scarf just using your arms and our Crazy Sexy Wool. We call it the Moby Scarf. This tutorial is real time so you should be able to follow along easily. Learn to cast on and work basic stitches and cast off to create a giant comfy scarf for yourself or a friend.

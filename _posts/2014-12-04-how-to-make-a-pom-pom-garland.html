[stag_intro]Yarn isn't just for knitting...why not use it for getting into the festive spirit? Knitter or not, you can easily customise your house, office and gifts with our pom-pom garland.
[/stag_intro]

The countdown to Christmas has begun: advent calendars opened, tree up and mulled wine slowly getting us through the knitting gift list. Fancy a welcome break from making your mother-in-law that scarf? Our pom-pom garland is a fun and funky way to put a little Wool and the Gang into your grotto.

The best bit about our pom-pom garland is that it uses up all your loose ends and will last you for festive seasons to come, plus it's so simple that everyone can get involved - they don't even need to be knitters! You can make a large garland using our Crazy Sexy Wool, the bigger garlands are great for decorating interiors or your Christmas tree. Our Sugar Baby Alpaca is ideal for creating a smaller garland, which is perfect for any stockings and even adding final touches to your presents.

So, here's how you do it. Firstly, dig into that stash of leftover yarns and pick out the colours you want for your garland:

Then make as many pom-pom's as you can! Our easy tutorial can show you how to make a pom-pom in just 3 minutes.

[stag_intro]WATG TAKE OVER THE ROOFTOP AT THE QUEEN OF HOXTON....[/stag_intro]

Last weekend, The Gang teamed up with The Queen of Hoxton to run our first Jewellery Workshop. All the Gangstas learnt how to knit the Sansa Choker and Khaleesi Bracelet, leaving with one or two chokers and bracelets in their choice of vibrant and bold Jersey Be Good colours. What better way to spend a Saturday than to learn something new, make new friends and enjoy the London views with a drink in hand?

[stag_intro]Food, music, wool. What could be a better combo? So of course The Gang were mega-excited to be invited to wool-jazzle The Cheese Hub at Jamie Oliver and Alex James's The Big Feastival.[/stag_intro]

The brief was simple - this year's theme for Alex James's* Cheese Hub was BIG, BOLD and WOOLLY!  So who better to deliver than...well, US?!

The Gang got our (woolly) thinking caps on to create a world of woollen wonders using our Crazy Sexy Wool and Jersey Be Good yarns. Cue crochet parasols, woolly chandeliers, Crazy Sexy cheeseplants and knotty-but-nice wall-hangings. Here's a sneak peek behind the scenes - part madness, part method. If it looks like fun, that's because it was!

We're off to The Big Feastival tomorrow - we'll be running a host of Wool and the Gang workshops and selling our woolly wares. Come and say hello if you're festival-bound - and be sure to check out The Cheese Hub!

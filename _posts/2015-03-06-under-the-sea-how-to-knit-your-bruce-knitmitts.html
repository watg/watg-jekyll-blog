You've seen them on the runway at London Fashion Week - now snap up a Bruce KnitMitts Kit and make a pair of your very own finned friends. Designed by Wool and the Gang in collaboration with iconic British designer Christopher Ræburn, this is the ultimate sustainable fashion mash-up.

No need to fish for compliments - your chunky shark mitts will reel them in.

Our jawsome friends are knitted in the round on 12mm double-pointed needles, using Wool and the Gang's Crazy Sexy Wool. If this is a new technique for you, worry not - we've put together a video tutorial to get you up to speed:[stag_video src="http://youtu.be/hcj5ioQYbtE"]

To create Bruce's jaws you'll need to divide your stitches by popping some of them on hold - here's how:[stag_video src="http://youtu.be/83h4XYCpSfI"]

The inside of Bruce's jaw is created using the duplicate stitch technique - it's a firm WATG favourite. Check out our video if you need a helping hand.

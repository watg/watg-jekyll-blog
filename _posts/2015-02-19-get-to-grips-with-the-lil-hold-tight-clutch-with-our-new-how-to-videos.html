The miniature off-spring of our ever-popular Hold Tight Clutch, the Lil' Hold Tight Clutch is small but perfectly formed, for days when you just wanna travel light with the bare essentials. Whether you choose to buy one ready-made by our superstar Gangstas, or grab your sticks and knit your own bag from our awesome Jersey Be Good yarn, we guarantee you'll want one to go with every outfit!

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2015/02/Lil_HTC_HandSolo.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2015/02/LHTC-knit-along.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]
To celebrate the launch our new favourite bag-ette, we’re holding our first Wool and the Gang Knit-Along. Join us on the weekend of 28th February to 1st March and knit your very on Lil’ one!

We’ve put together a series of video tutorials to guide you through the process and will be on hand to answer  your questions along the way.  We'll also show you how to pimp up your Lil' one and add your own personal touches to make your bag truly #madeunique.

[stag_intro]

Hey Gangstas, Here's a step by step guide that shows you how easy peasy it is to attach your jewellery clasps to either the Sansa Choker or Khaleesi Braclet.
Let's get down on knit...

[/stag_intro]

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/1_3.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

The Tools

Lobster Clasp
Tassel End
Teeny tiny screw
Scissors (not provided in the kit)

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/2_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

SO LETS GO! FIRST OF ALL. TIE THE TAILS OF YARN INTO A TIGHT KNOT.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/3_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

REMOVE THE TINY SCREWS FROM THE LOBSTER CLASP.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/4_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

PUSH THE THE END OF THE JEWELLERY INTO THE CLASP. USING A BIT OF ELBOW GREASE USE THE SCREW DRIVER TO PUSH AS MUCH OF THE END INTO THE SOCKET.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/5_.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Keep going!

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/6_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Now use your screw and screw in using the screw driver…like so :)

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/7_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/8_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Keep going, so it feels secure!

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/9_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Tug it to make sure it is secure. Now trim any ends…

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/10_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

…And tuck them up in to the clasp using the screw driver.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/11_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

NOW REPEAT THE STEPS ABOVE TO ATTACH THE TASSEL END TO THE OTHER END OF THE JEWELLERY PIECE.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/12_1.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

TAAAA DAAA! You're done.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/05/13_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

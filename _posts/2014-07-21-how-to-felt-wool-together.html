[stag_intro]Time to master the magical technique of joining your balls of yarn by felting them together. No more sewing in those ends! [/stag_intro]

The Tools:

 - Crazy Sexy Wool

- Water

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/1.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Start with the end of your old yarn, and the beginning of your new ball.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/2.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Dip both ends of the yarn in the water and get them fully wet.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/3.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Squeeze the extra water out.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="http://watg-blog.s3.amazon[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/7_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]aws.com/2014/07/4.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Overlap the two ends in your hands by 3 inches.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/5.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Rub the ends together between your palms...up and down...

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/6.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Keep going!

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/7_0.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

You may need to get them wet again....

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/9.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

Make sure that all of the ends are felted in.

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/10.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

TAAAA-DAAAA! Like magic they are now joined together :)

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/07/11.jpg" alignment="center" url=""]

You are ready to knit with your new ball of yarn. No weaving them in later! Hoorah!

This only works with some animal fibres, typically pure wool is best. Perfect for when you are knitting with our Crazy Sexy Wool!

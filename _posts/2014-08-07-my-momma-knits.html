We’re feeling a bit broody here at Wool HQ, we had an awesome day knitting with some London Mommas and their Baby Gangstas at iconic landmark Battersea Power Station.(met through super-cool organisation Mothers Meetings)

Needless to say, our wool school quickly turned to drool school as the Baby Gang watched their mums knit up a storm. There was laughter, there were tears (and only a few teething tantrums) but by the end of our workshop the mums had knitted The Sansa Choker and were already seeking their next project. Here are some snaps from the day, so much respect to these Super Women - they taught us a thing or two about multi-tasking!

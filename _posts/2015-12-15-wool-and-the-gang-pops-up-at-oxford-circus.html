Wool and the Gang pop-up @ Oxford Circus tube station
17th to 24th December

Oh yes, the rumours are true! We are going to be popping up at Oxford Circus tube station for one week before Christmas, hopefully perfectly timed for all of you out there who just *might* have left your gift-shopping a liiiiittle late. We'll be stocking some of our classic styles as ready made pieces and as knit kits. Plus, every lunch time there's a half hour pompom keyring workshop and all week there's a chance to win a giant, pretty-much-human-sized Alpacino soft toy too!

Opening dates and times

Thursday 17th Dec - 7am to 9pm
Friday 18th Dec - 7am to 9pm
Saturday 19th Dec - 10am to 3pm
Sunday 20th Dec - 11am to 3pm
Monday 21st Dec - 7am to 9pm
Tuesday 22nd Dec - 7am to 9pm
Wednesday 23rd Dec - 7am to 9pm
Thursday 24th Dec - 9am to 3pm

Where

Where you come out of the exit turnstiles at Oxford Circus tube station (where the WH Smith used to be, for all you regulars). You can access it on your way out of the tube station, or by going down the stairs at the side of the station on Argyll Street.

Pompom keyring workshops

Every day from 1-1.30pm
£8 per person
All components included. No prior experience whatsoever needed!
Book your spot here.

At the end of your workshop you'll walk out with one of these beauties - perfect as a gift, but also good enough to keep all for yourself!

Win 'Big Al' (AKA our pretty-much-human-sized Alpacino soft toy)

We know you all loved our Alpacino soft toy, so we super-sized him - he's now approximately 1m 50 cm or 4 foot 9 inches!  We'll have Big Al with us in the shop and all you need to do to enter to stand a chance of winning him is pose for a selfie with him, pop it up on Instagram and tag @woolandthegang and hashtag #woolandthegang as well. We'll announce the winner on the 27th December and will ship to a London address only.

 

Hope to see you there!

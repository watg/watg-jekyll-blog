[stag_intro]Please meet Bonne Maman, Co Founder Aurelie's knitting wizard of a Grandma.[/stag_intro]

Like the jam she is sweet, cheers you up when you are down and is always there when you need her. Like most of us, our Grandma's taught us to knit so Aurelie stopped by her cosy home to show us her Grandma's love for knitting and her stashes of patterns, scissors and yarn that make us green with envy!

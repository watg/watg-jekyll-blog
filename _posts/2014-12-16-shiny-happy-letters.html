This Christmas we are getting personal. Just in time for Santa’s sleigh we're launching our new ABC Silver Badges.

Here at the Gang we love to customise our knitwear, from our Ashleigh Sweater to our Count On Me and Say My Name beanies. After our recent collaboration with Whistles we decided to find other ways we could make our knits fun and unique. It didn't take our design team long to think sexy, shiny and sparkly.

Made in the UK, our ABC Silver Badges are light-weight and easy to wear. Spell out your initials, name or even an 'OMG' or 'WATG'. Each mirrored letter is a pin badge so it’s really simple to take them from your jumper and put them onto your hat, bag, scarf, coat…anything really. We also think our ABC Silver Badges make great presents, slip one into your friend's stocking or a Secret Santa to give a gift that will keep giving (and sparkling) all year round.  You could also decorate your home with our silver letters: they add cool, individual touches to cushions and kids bedrooms. Show us your silver with #Shareyourknits.

Make it unique, make it personal.

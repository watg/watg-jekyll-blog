Emma is a London based knitwear designer, working for Knitwear brands such as Yulia Kondranina. Favoring natural, luxurious fibers Emma loves to work with our colourful Crazy Sexy Wool.

[/stag_intro]

[stag_image style="no-filter" src="https://watg-blog.s3.amazonaws.com/2014/12/X.jpg" alignment="none" url=""]

 

Emma was one of our first gang makers, helping us knit up your favorite products since the beginning of our knitting revolution. We are lucky enough to share our maker Emma with top Knitwear designers, which is where she spends her time when she isn't in her King's Cross home. On a frosty monday morning we got the chance to pop over to her cozy apartment for a coffee, cake and a chat about all things woolly.

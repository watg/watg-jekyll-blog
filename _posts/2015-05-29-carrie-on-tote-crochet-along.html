[stag_intro]We're gonna get you hooked this June as we launch our very first crochet along! [/stag_intro]

We're going to join forces to make the Carrie on Tote. Our crochet along will run from 13th June, and all you'll need to get started is a Carrie on Tote Kit.

To join in, just order your supplies, wait patiently for them to arrive then, from the 13th of June, crochet along! Share your journey on social media - our favourite crochet journal will win an Pouf Panther Kit in a colour of your choice. Just tag any posts with #crochetwiththegang so we see how you're getting on. If you're sharing on a blog, pop the URL in the comments below.

WHAT'S A CROCHET ALONG?

It's simple - it's a group of people getting together to crochet a project, to share their experiences, and to help each other along the way. Sounds great, right? As our Gang are spread across the globe, we'll be coming together online. You can join in on Facebook, Instagram, Twitter and Ravelry - our hashtag is #crochetwiththegang.

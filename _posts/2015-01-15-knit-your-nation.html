[stag_intro]We've given our Zion Lion a makeover to be proud of - you can now embroider your favourite flag onto your Wool and the Gang beanie. Our founding Gangstas Jade and Aurelie grabbed their skis and tested out their new beanies in the magical Swiss Alps. 
[/stag_intro]

Made from our finest 100% Peruvian Crazy Sexy Wool, our Flag Beanies are knitted by our team of Gangstas. The Zion Lion base is super-easy to knit and its oversized shape will keep you extra warm - perfect for cold mornings and chilly nights. At WATG we like our accessories to make a stylish statement and as temperatures drop we feel it's time to update our headgear. Although we love our Zion Lion Poms and our monogrammed Say My Name hats, our new Flag Beanies are just what our winter wardrobe needed. Knit it yourself or buy one ready-made by one of our Gangsta knitters. We've picked some of our favourite flag designs - Let It Beanie, Born In The USA Beanie, Zermatt Beanie, Bellevillie Beanie, Soul to Seoul Beanie and Harajuku Beanie - and in case you need some ski style inspiration, here are some of the Gang flying their flags on the Swiss slopes.

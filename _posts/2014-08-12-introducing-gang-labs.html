At WATG, we've always known what a creative bunch our Gang are - we wanted to make the most of it so we decided to invite them to collaborate with us on some new designs. That's how “Gang Labs” was born, a new fashion line, all designed by the Gang.

The first step was to design a bag featuring our brand new Mixtape yarn using our special clasps, we were blown away by all the amazing design ideas we received and after picking our Top Ten, we invited everyone to vote for their favourites.

We are so excited to introduce you to the winning designs that are now WATG Knit Kits!

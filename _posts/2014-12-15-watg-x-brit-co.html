We have teamed up with maker community Brit + Co to bring you an exclusive online knitting class, hosted by our co-founder Jade. You could learn how to knit a sexy and stylish snood in under 70 minutes! 

Just like us, Brit + Co is an online community of makers. Inspired by the maker movement, Brit + Co was started to get people creating, learning and sharing.  On their digital site you can explore, make, learn and shop all different kinds of crafts, from fashion to literature to baking. Brit + Co aim to encourage people to use technology to become more creative in the real world, using sustainable and ethical materials and processes, sound familiar?

Brit + Co has a wide range of DIY e-classes, so we sent along our co-founder Jade Harwood to give an exclusive beginners class on knitting.  This basic knitting class will get you up and stitching in just 30 minutes. Jade will teach you how to hold the needles, cast on and the simple knitting stitches you will need, as well as some tips and tricks for common knitting problems. By the end of the 70-minute class you will have learnt how to make your very own Wild Spirit Snood. This fun, easy class costs just $19.99, plus when you buy the kit with the class you'll get a $5 discount! Our stylish Wild Spirit Snood is a great kit for any new knitters. Making the class a perfect present for your friends or a Christmas treat for yourself.
